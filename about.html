Who we are...

We are a General Contractor specializing in Metal Buildings. We Started in August of 2010.  Although our specialty is metal buildings, We are capable of performing all types of construction.

We have long ties to the community and Western Montana. We take great pride in our workmanship. Our ultimate goal isn't to just complete the project, but to complete the project with complete customer satisfaction.

R & R Shear Construction is a licensed and insured member of the Montana Contractors Association.
